# Connect and Configure Data for your AppSheet App

<h2>📋 Steps</h2>

<ol>
  <li>
    Open the provided <b>Customer Contacts</b> AppSheet app link.
  </li>

  <li>
    Click <b>Copy app</b> from the left sidebar.
  </li>

  <li>
    Configure the application:
    <ul>
      <li><b>App Name:</b> Customer Contacts</li>
      <li>Leave all other settings as their default values.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>

  <li>
    Click <b>Copy app</b> and wait for the application to be copied to your AppSheet account.
  </li>

  <li>
    Open the provided <b>Companies</b> Google Sheets link.
  </li>

  <li>
    Click <b>Make a copy</b> to save the spreadsheet to your Google Drive.
  </li>

  <li>
    Rename the copied spreadsheet to <b>companies</b>.
  </li>

  <li>
    Download the <b>contacts.xlsx</b> file.
  </li>

  <li>
    Click <b>Upload</b> and select the <b>contacts.xlsx</b> file.
  </li>

  <li>
    Click <b>Import Data</b>.
  </li>

</ol>
